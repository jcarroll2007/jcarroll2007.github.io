Flavors

At Downtown Scoops we serve Jake's homemade ice cream that is made right here in Atlanta, GA. We rotate our selection across Jake's 200 flavors daily, so make sure you visit often to try our new additions. Don't worry though, we keep our favorites around. Like "Chocolate Slap Yo Mama" and "Brown Shugah Vanilla". See the list below for just a few of the flavors that we serve.

Vanillas
Brown Shugah Vanilla™– A very special vanilla made with brown sugar…our second most popular flavor!
Honey Vanilla– It’s the bee’s knees…that’s the buzz when natural golden sweetness meets tropical orchid.
 Yo Daddy’s Vanilla– After the lawn was mowed, the game was watched, and the churn was turned there was… Yo Daddy’s Vanilla. Classic sweet cream base and pure vanilla.
 Steve’s Thrillah Vanilla™– Named after our bud Steve Schickler, former President & CEO of Edy’s-Dreyer’s Ice Cream- it’s replete with bean specks – Thanks Steve!
 Vanilla Malt– Jake’s personal favorite, like an old fashioned malted the way you remember.

Chocolates
Chocolate Slap Yo Mama™ – Made with our signature chocolate sauce, chocolate chips and oreo cookies, this is the black sheep of cookies and cream. And it’s our most popular seller. Do NOT cuss your preacher!
Chocolate Cinnamon– Mmmmilk chocolate ice cream with a wisp of cinnamon.
Chocolate Raspberry Truffle– Who knew ice cream could be so decadent? Raspberries and chocolate dancing for joy in this sinful flavor.
Chocolate Peanut Butter– Our rich chocolate base with chocolate peanut butter cups and gobs of luscious peanut butter and more peanut butter cups and more…
Chocolate Pecan Piescream™– An entire pecan pie mixed into a chocolate base—a great twist on a Southern favorite!
Chocotella™– Hazelnut spread throughout our chocolate ice cream. We must be nuts…tell a friend.
German Chocolate Cakescream– A Bavarian treat made with coconut and pecan frosting, and lots and lots of chocolate.
Hot Sexy Mexican™– Sweet chocolate cream with a cayenne kick. The Aztecs would be so proud!
The Rockiest Road™– Our road is bumpier and rockier, with more marshmallows, pecans and chocolate chips than the DOT allows.
Thin Mint– That classic Girl Scout cookie flavor swirled into classic Jake’s chocolate ice cream. Some traditions shouldn’t be messed with.
Jamocha Pecan Chip– Jake’s take on an old favorite. Chocolate ice cream jammin’ with coffee, pecans and chocolate chips.

Coffees
Cinnamon Kenya AA– Wake up and taste the coffee… and cinnamon! Made with ground espresso coffee beans, a hint of cinnamon and a splash of vanilla.
Coffee & Donuts™– Hot, fresh, now doughnuts dunked in coffee liqueur gone swimming in coffee ice cream.
Coffee Toffee – Coffee ice cream with more than a bit of Heath Bar bits.
Diesel Fuel– Our chocolate syrup in coffee ice cream, a dash of cinnamon and chocolate covered espresso beans for a delicious jolt.
Kenya AA– Wake up and taste the coffee… Made with fresh ground espresso coffee beans and a splash of vanilla HELLO!
Tiramisu Su Sudio™– To date, the most popular Flavah of the Month winner in Jake’s history. Coffee, more coffee, mascarpone cream and lady fingers…if that don’t pick you up, what will?

Caramels
Salted Caramel – Before it became all the rage, it was outrageous at Jake’s! We melt cane sugar to a gorgeous amber, add fresh cream until it’s perfection, then make our ice cream surround it and then sea salt…just enough for the salty sweet flavor.
Betwixed™– – And between, plenty of those yummy, crispy, chewy candy bars.
Delta’s Delight– created especially for Delta Air Lines with Biscoff cookies, caramel swirls and Irish cream whiskey. Jake’s way of saying thanks for 75 great years of customer service!
Elton’s Good Karma’l™– Rich caramel ice cream with wee bit of Irish cream whiskey mixed in. The universe just got a little more balanced.
